Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 4, 2025

At AutoCleanInbox, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

Information We Access

  • We access your Gmail inbox only after you grant explicit permission via Google OAuth 2.0.
  • We only process the metadata and content required for spam detection and inbox organization.

Data Storage

No emails are permanently stored. All processing happens in real time, and temporary data is cleared immediately.

Third-Party Access

We do not share your data with third parties. The app only uses Google APIs for email access.

User Control

You can revoke access anytime from your Google Account settings.
